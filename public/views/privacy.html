Terms and Conditions:-

As per Affordable Residential Housing Scheme under Rajasthan Jan Awas Yojana 2015 Policy. All non-successful applicants, shall be refunded back the booking amount within 30 days of holding the draw of lots.


Once the applications relating to allotment of flats in the Project, are received by the Company, the same shall be scrutinized. Scrutiny of applications shall be completed by the Company. Applications found to be ineligible shall be returned alongwith the Registration Amount (Rs. 21,000/-) received from such applicants. No interest shall be paid in such cases.


Allotment of flats in the Project shall be made by way of draw of lots.


Upto 5% of the total number of flats as approved in the site layout plans may be allotted by the Company to its employees/associates/friends/relatives etc.


Only such Applications shall be considered for draw of lots which are complete. The list of such successful allottees shall also be published on this website.


Cancellation and Refunds:-

A waiting list shall also be prepared during the draw of lots who can be offered the allotment in case some of the successful allottees are not able to provide documents as per the scheme published in the advertisement. All waiting and non-successful applicants shall be refunded back the Booking Amount within 45 days of holding the draw of lots.


Only such Applications shall be considered for draw of lots which are complete and which fulfill the criteria given under the scheme published.
