    • How To Apply

    • Choose the project from the 'LIVE' on the home page
    • Click on 'Project Scheme Details'
    • Check all the approvals, licences, layouts, floor plans, location, and other specifications of the selected project
    • Click on 'Apply Online'
    • Fill the form completely and click on 'Submit and Pay' at the bottom of the form
    • You will be taken to the payment page, where you need to fill in some details and need to select the mode of payment from the available options as per your preference. Then click 'Make Payment', enter OTP, and your registration is done
    • You will get the registration confirmation and application number via text/email
